THE SNIPER // 11-STEP PRECISION PLAYBOOK
  • 1. IDENTIFY THE WALL: Wait for price to touch a clear Floor or Ceiling.
  • 2. CONFIRM EXTREME: Verify the 15M Gauge is "Strong" (pegged at 80/20).
  • 3. WAIT FOR DRIFT: Let price pull away from the wall; remain patient.
  • 4. MONITOR THE ROLL: Watch the gauge drift back into the session-neutral zone.
  • 5. DYNAMIC RANGE RULE: ALWAYS check the range label. It filters out market noise.
  • 6. PREPARE FOR CROSS: Watch the Fast Blue Line (K) and Slow Orange Line (D) from 6440.jpg. Wait for them to head toward each other.
  • 7. TRIGGER CONFIRMATION: Buy when Blue crosses UP over Orange; Sell when Blue crosses DOWN under Orange, inside the neutral zone.
  • 8. EXECUTE & LOCK: Click trade exactly at the crossover, then hit LOCK & LOG.
  • 9. THE NO-TRADE PROTOCOL: If the lines don't cross cleanly, or market noise is high: ABORT. Do not force it.
  • 10. STAY READY: Keep your trading app open and "at the ready" from Step 6.
  • 11. THE EXIT STRATEGY: Profit at the next opposing Wall or if indicator lines cross opposite. Cut loss if the price breaks decisively through your original Wall.
